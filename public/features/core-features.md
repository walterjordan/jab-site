# Platform Features Overview

## Conversations
Manage all of your customer communication channels (Email, SMS, DM, Calls, & Reviews) in one place.

---

## Communication Tools

### Chat
Live chat with your customers via SMS to increase engagement and drive more sales.

### Calling
Get a dedicated phone number to make outbound calls, receive inbound calls, and send/receive texts.

### SMS Text
Send SMS messages to your customers to improve reach and response rates.

---

## Payments & Revenue

### Payments
Accept payments for products and services. Send invoices and enable text-to-pay options.

### Products
Create products and services easily while tracking sales performance.

### Affiliate Management
Manage affiliates, campaigns, payouts, and promote your products and services.

---

## Customer Management

### CRM
Manage contacts, companies, leads, and customers in a centralized system.

### Pipelines
Track sales, opportunities, and customer journeys through customizable pipelines.

### Reputation Management
Send automated review requests, track reviews, and dispute negative feedback efficiently.

---

## Scheduling & Booking

### Calendars
Create and manage calendars to organize your availability and increase bookings.

### Appointments
Allow customers to book appointments and optionally collect payments during booking.

---

## Marketing Tools

### Email Marketing
Send automated emails or broadcast campaigns using a drag-and-drop email builder.

### Funnels
Build high-converting funnels using an intuitive drag-and-drop editor.

### Websites
Create full websites with an easy-to-use drag-and-drop builder.

### Social Posting
Post content and manage comments across Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok.

### Templates
Access ready-made templates for websites, funnels, emails, and LinkTree-style pages.

---
